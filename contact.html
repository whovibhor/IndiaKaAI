Frequently Asked Questions

How can I contribute content?

We welcome contributions from the community! You can submit articles, tutorials, or project showcases through our content submission form. Our team reviews all submissions to ensure quality and relevance.

Do you offer partnerships?

Yes! We're always looking for partners who share our vision of democratizing AI education in India. Whether you're an educational institution, tech company, or community organization, we'd love to explore collaboration opportunities.

How can I join the community?

Join our Discord server or Telegram group to connect with other AI enthusiasts and stay updated with our activities. We host regular events, workshops, and discussions to foster learning and collaboration.

Are the learning resources free?

Yes! All our learning resources, including courses, tutorials, and project guides, are completely free. We believe in making AI education accessible to everyone in India.

How can I get help with my AI project?

You can get help through our community forums, Discord server, or by attending our regular office hours. We also have a mentorship program where experienced AI practitioners guide beginners.

Do you offer certificates?

Yes, we provide certificates of completion for our learning paths and courses. These certificates are recognized by our partner organizations and can be added to your professional profile.
